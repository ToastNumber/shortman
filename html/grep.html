grep

grep [options] pattern [files]

Searches the files for the pattern.

grep "\\(b\\|c\\|r\\)"at *.java
finds occurrences of bat, cat, or rat in .java files

grep -r "dog" *.java finds occurrences of the pattern cat in *.java files in all sub-directories

grep -i 'dog' *.java finds occurrences ignoring case
